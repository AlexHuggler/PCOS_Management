Frequently asked questions

Is CycleBalance free to use?

CycleBalance is free to download and includes core tracking features.

Can I use it if my cycles are very irregular?

Yes. It is designed for irregular cycles and PCOS-aware tracking.

Is my data private and secure?

Your health logs stay on your device unless you choose to export them.

Does CycleBalance replace medical care?

No. CycleBalance supports tracking and education, not diagnosis or treatment.
