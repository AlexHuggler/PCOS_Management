2. Description of Service

CycleBalance is an iOS health and wellness application designed specifically for individuals managing Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS). The App provides the following features:

  • Cycle Tracking: Tools for tracking irregular menstrual cycles without relying on standard 28-day assumptions.
  • Symptom Logging: The ability to record and monitor PCOS-related symptoms, including hormonal changes, insulin and glucose levels, dietary impact, and other health markers.
  • Pregnancy and Postpartum Tracking: Optional tracking for pregnancy and postpartum periods, including estimated due dates and pregnancy outcomes.
  • HealthKit Integration: Optional read-only access to Apple HealthKit data, including body mass, sleep analysis, blood glucose, active energy, resting heart rate, and step count, to provide a more comprehensive health picture.
  • On-Device Machine Learning Insights: Personalized pattern recognition and correlations generated entirely on your device using on-device ML, designed to help you better understand your body's unique patterns.
  • Health Reports and Data Export: PDF health reports for sharing with your healthcare provider, CSV data exports for spreadsheet use, and JSON backups with schema versioning for full data portability.

We may update, modify, or discontinue features of the App at any time without prior notice.

8. HealthKit Integration

CycleBalance offers optional integration with Apple HealthKit to provide a more comprehensive view of your health data. By granting permission, you allow the App to read the following data types from HealthKit:

  • Body mass (weight)
  • Sleep analysis
  • Blood glucose
  • Resting heart rate
  • Active energy burned
  • Step count

HealthKit integration is entirely optional. You can enable or disable access to each data type individually through your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > CycleBalance.

CycleBalance accesses HealthKit data in read-only mode. The App does not write any data to HealthKit.

All HealthKit data accessed by CycleBalance remains on your device and is used solely within the App to display health metrics and generate insights. HealthKit data is never transmitted to CycleBalance servers, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.
