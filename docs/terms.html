2. Description of Service

CycleBalance is an iOS health and wellness application designed specifically for individuals managing Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS). The App provides the following features:

  • Cycle Tracking: Tools for tracking irregular menstrual cycles without relying on standard 28-day assumptions.
  • Symptom Logging: The ability to record and monitor PCOS-related symptoms, including hormonal changes, insulin and glucose levels, dietary impact, and other health markers.
  • On-Device Machine Learning Insights: Personalized pattern recognition and correlations generated entirely on your device using on-device ML, designed to help you better understand your body's unique patterns.
  • Doctor-Ready Reports: Exportable summaries of your tracked data to share with your healthcare provider.
