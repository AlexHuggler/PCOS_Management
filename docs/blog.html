Every major cycle tracking app on the market assumes a 28-day cycle. If your period is irregular, late, or unpredictable, you get red warnings, confused predictions, and features that simply don't apply. PCOS Education March 2026

Understanding Irregular Cycles with PCOS

Polycystic ovary syndrome disrupts the hormonal signals that drive ovulation, which means cycles can range anywhere from 21 days to several months. Tracking these patterns over time is one of the most powerful things you can do to understand your body and have more productive conversations with your doctor.
